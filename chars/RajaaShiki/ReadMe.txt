===============| Movelist - Legend |===============

x    = Weak Punch
y    = Medium Punch
z    = Strong Punch
a    = Weak Kick
b    = Medium Kick
c    = Strong Kick

U    = Up
D    = Down
F    = Forward
B    = Back

P    = Any Punch
K    = Any Kick

QCF  = Quarter Circle Forward
QCB  = Quarter Circle Back
HCF  = Half Circle Forward
HCB  = Half Circle Back

DPM  = Forward, Down, Forward (Dragon Punch motion, you know shoryuken? =[ ...Ryu..Ken...)
DPMB = Back, Down, Back (Dragon punch motion backwards)

B->F = Hold back then press forward
D->U = Hold down then press up

PPP  = Repeatedly Press Punch buttons
KKK  = Repeatedly Press Kick buttons

===============| Movelist |===============

<<<< SPECIALS >>>>

- Back Flip        = B     ; Done after landing from a back dash.

- Sucker Punch     = F+y   ; Shiki punches the opponent.

- Back Fist        = F+z   ; Shiki back hands the opponent. Can be used for linking.

- See No Evil      = F+a*3 ; Hold forward and press the "a" button up to three times.

- Surprise Gash    = b     ; Can only be done after performing a medium kick with the b button.

- Tackle           = F+c   ; Shiki tackles the opponent with his shoulder.

- Winch Gut        = QCF+P ; Shiki dashes forwards and attempts to slash the opponent.

- Reverse Heel     = DPM+K ; Shiki does an upward kick. Use a follow up attack by pressing or holding the kick button.

- Mole Sting       = QCB+K ; Shiki slides under high attacks and attempts to trip his opponent. For the C version, press C again if the move hits to perform a launcher.

- Shiki's Blitz    = QCB+P ; Shiki gets into position to do various moves depending on the button pressed.

- Thousand Slashes = PPP   ; Shiki repeatedly strikes for as long as you can keep pressing the button.

- Hornet's Sting   = QCF+P ; Can only be performed in the air. Shiki aims down and stings the opponent from above.

<<<< SUPERS >>>>

*Each Super special has up to 3 Levels, unless specified otherwise*

- Double Gut               = QCF*2+P ; Shiki does two quick slashes. Looks cool and is great for combos.

- Soaring Graze            = QCB*2+P ; Shiki gets into position to move at super speed to strike an unexpected opponent from above.

- Hornet's Nest            = QCF*2+P ; Can only be performed in the air. Shiki aims down and stings the opponent from above.

- Inverse Heel             = QCF*2+K ; Needs 2 power stocks. Shiki attempts a flying kick to his opponent and performs two extra hits if he connects.

- Shadow Slash             = D*3+P   ; Needs 2 power stocks. Shiki counters any attack except projectile attacks. Looks cool.

- Eyes of Death Perception = F>HCF+P ; Needs 3 power stocks. Shiki is able to see things in slow motion. Looks cool and is very useful.

===============| System Moves |=================

- Run                = FF

- Back-dash          = BB

- Evasion            = x+a+F or x+a+B

- Dodge              = x+a

- Shield             = x+a (only during vigormode)

- High Jump          = DU

- Air Jump           = U (Can Air-Jump cancel from selected air attacks)

- Air Dash           = FF (Can Air-Dash cancel from selected air attacks)

- Jump cancel        = Perform a jump or high jump during some moves.

- Recovery           = x+a (When you're hit and falling and recovering is possible)

- Grab               = F/B + two Punches/Kicks 

- Airborne Grab      = F/B + two Punches/Kicks 

- Power Charge       = y+b

- Life charge        = y+b (only during vigormode)

- Parry              = Fwd for Standing/Airbourne Attacks || Dwn for Crouching Attacks.

- Guard Counter      = y+b+F when hit while guarding. Needs at least one power bar.

- C-Groove Canceling = Can only be done with supers that have 3 levels. If the opponent is hit with the level 2 version of the given super, cancel that super into a special attack, or another super attack if there's enough power remaining.

- Charge Attacks     = for some attacks, hold the button for more damage/chip damage.

- Vigor Mode         = z+c (At least one level of power is needed to activate vigormode)

===============| Vigor Mode |=================

<<<< Pros >>>>

- Vigor Mode can be activated and deactivated on the ground or in the air.

- Normal-attack chaining is enabled (including air normals).

- Most Normal attacks can be jump canceled.

- All normal attacks cause air fall.

- All normal attacks give different air velocities to the opponent to facilitate juggling.

- Vigor special moves become available.

- Supermoves cannot be activated.

- Life-Charge replaces Power-Charge.

- Shield replaces Dodge.

- Reflects one attack during activation while on the ground.

- chargeable moves' charge times are sped up to make charge attacks more useful.

- Juggling capabilities are doubled.

- Recovertime for air normals are increased.

- Can chain into an air grab if air grab is available.

<<<< Cons >>>>

- Power is completed depleted and cannot increase until vigormode is exited.

- Cannot guard while in the air.

- Cannot parry.

- Cannot dodge forward or backward.

- Vigor specials are the only specials that can be performed.

- Vigor Specials and Fully charged charged moves deplete the power bar (the amount of depletion depends on the move).

- Cannot perform any supermoves.

- Life charge cannot be activated while the vigorbar is blinking.

- Cannot perform a low jump.

- Air chaining increases landing recover time.

===============| Useful Tips |===============

- During Vigor Mode, some moves may act differently.

- Shiki has more juggle and combo ability than any of my other characters.

- Sucker Punch can be charged to become an overhead.

- Surprise gash can be charged for a launcher. You can jump cancel to perform an air attack after the launcher.

- Tackle can be charged to become a knock down. The normal version of Tackle can also be canceled into most basic, special and super attacks.

- Jumping Strong kick can be charged to make the opponent bounce into the air for air combo possibilities.

- Mole Sting's extra attack can be jump canceled.

- The light version of Shiki's Blitz can be charged to become an overhead.

- Eyes of Death Perception is unguardable for the first two ticks of the the red hitbox's appearance.
