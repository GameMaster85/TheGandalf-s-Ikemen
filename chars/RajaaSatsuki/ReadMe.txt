===============| Movelist - Legend |===============

x    = Weak Punch
y    = Medium Punch
z    = Strong Punch
a    = Weak Kick
b    = Medium Kick
c    = Strong Kick

U    = Up
D    = Down
F    = Forward
B    = Back

P    = Any Punch
K    = Any Kick

QCF  = Quarter Circle Forward
QCB  = Quarter Circle Back
HCF  = Half Circle Forward
HCB  = Half Circle Back

DPM  = Forward, Down, Forward (Dragon Punch motion, you know shoryuken? =[ ...Ryu..Ken...)
DPMB = Back, Down, Back (Dragon punch motion backwards)

B->F = Hold back then press forward
D->U = Hold down then press up

PPP  = Repeatedly Press Punch buttons
KKK  = Repeatedly Press Kick buttons

===============| Movelist |===============

<<<< SPECIALS >>>>

- Cry Baby     = F+x*3  ; Hold forward and press the "x" button up to three times.

- Pounder Wave = QCF+K  ; Satsuki punches the ground and a wave of energy sweeps the ground.

- Windup Punch = QCB+P  ; Satsuki winds up her arm and then punches. The medium and high versions can absorb attacks on start up and can be held.

- Arm Grasping = QCF+P  ; Satsuki reaches forward to grab grounded opponents.

- Arm Chasing  = DPM+P  ; Satsuki reaches forward to grab airborne opponents.

- Slam Dunk    = DPMB+P ; Satsuki jumps upward to grab airborne opponents.

<<<< SUPERS >>>>

*Each Super special has up to 3 Levels, unless specified otherwise*

- Excessive Pounder Wave = QCF*2+K ; Satsuki punches the ground multiple times and a waves of energies sweep the ground.

- Dark Heart             = QCB*2+P ; Satsuki dashes forward to grab the opponent heart.

- EX Bite                = QCF*2+P ; Needs 2 power stocks. Satsuki attempts to grab the opponent and suck their blood. Deals damage and heals health.

- Depletion Garden       = F>HCF+P ; Needs 3 power stocks. Satsuki stops any physical attack and counters!

===============| System Moves |=================

- Run                = FF

- Back-dash          = BB

- Evasion            = x+a+F or x+a+B

- Dodge              = x+a

- Shield             = x+a (only during vigormode)

- High Jump          = DU

- Air Jump           = U (Can Air-Jump cancel from selected air attacks)

- Air Dash           = FF (Can Air-Dash cancel from selected air attacks)

- Jump cancel        = Perform a jump or high jump during some moves.

- Recovery           = x+a (When you're hit and falling and recovering is possible)

- Grab               = F/B + two Punches/Kicks 

- Airborne Grab      = F/B + two Punches/Kicks 

- Power Charge       = y+b

- Life charge        = y+b (only during vigormode)

- Parry              = Fwd for Standing/Airbourne Attacks || Dwn for Crouching Attacks.

- Guard Counter      = y+b+F when hit while guarding. Needs at least one power bar.

- C-Groove Canceling = Can only be done with supers that have 3 levels. If the opponent is hit with the level 2 version of the given super, cancel that super into a special attack, or another super attack if there's enough power remaining.

- Charge Attacks     = for some attacks, hold the button for more damage/chip damage.

- Vigor Mode         = z+c (At least one level of power is needed to activate vigormode)

===============| Vigor Mode |=================

<<<< Pros >>>>

- Vigor Mode can be activated and deactivated on the ground or in the air.

- Normal-attack chaining is enabled (including air normals).

- Most Normal attacks can be jump canceled.

- All normal attacks cause air fall.

- All normal attacks give different air velocities to the opponent to facilitate juggling.

- Vigor special moves become available.

- Supermoves cannot be activated.

- Life-Charge replaces Power-Charge.

- Shield replaces Dodge.

- Reflects one attack during activation while on the ground.

- chargeable moves' charge times are sped up to make charge attacks more useful.

- Juggling capabilities are doubled.

- Recovertime for air normals are increased.

- Can chain into an air grab if air grab is available.

<<<< Cons >>>>

- Power is completed depleted and cannot increase until vigormode is exited.

- Cannot guard while in the air.

- Cannot parry.

- Cannot dodge forward or backward.

- Vigor specials are the only specials that can be performed.

- Vigor Specials and Fully charged charged moves deplete the power bar (the amount of depletion depends on the move).

- Cannot perform any supermoves.

- Life charge cannot be activated while the vigorbar is blinking.

- Cannot perform a low jump.

- Air chaining increases landing recover time.

===============| Useful Tips |===============

- During Vigor Mode, some moves may act differently.

- Standing Strong Punch can be charged for jump cancel.

- Standing Strong Kick can be charged to be a launcher and an overhead.

- Crouching Strong Punch can be charged for more hits.

- Crouching Strong Kick can be charged for more distance.

- Air Strong Punch can be charged.

- Dark Heart does more damage to Shiki Tohno.
